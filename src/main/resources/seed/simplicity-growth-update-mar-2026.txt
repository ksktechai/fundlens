Simplicity Growth Fund — Quarterly Fund Update, period ended 31 March 2026

What happened to the fund this quarter
The fund returned 1.5% after fees and tax for the year to 31 March 2026, slightly behind
the market index return of 1.9%. As a passively managed fund, performance closely tracked
the underlying global and Australasian share indices, with currency hedging detracting
approximately 0.3 percentage points over the year.

Fees
The total annual fund charge is 0.29% of net asset value. There are no contribution,
withdrawal, or performance fees. Simplicity operates as a nonprofit manager.

What the fund invests in
Target investment mix: 63% international equities, 15% Australasian equities,
17% fixed interest, 5% cash. Actual mix at quarter end: 62.8% international equities,
15.1% Australasian equities, 17.0% fixed interest, 5.1% cash.

Key facts
Fund size: $1.9 billion. Number of investors: 65,000. Risk indicator: 4 out of 7.
The five-year average annual return after fees and tax is 7.8%.
